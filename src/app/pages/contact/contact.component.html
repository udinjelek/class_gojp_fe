Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center. Here you can find frequently asked questions and resources to help you with any issues.

How to Create an Account?

To sign up for an account, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Login" button located at the top right corner of the page.
  2. On the login page, scroll down to the bottom and click the "Don't have an account?" link.
  3. You will be redirected to the sign-up page where you can create a new account.
  4. Fill in all the required information as prompted.
  5. Once you've entered all the necessary details, click the "Sign Up" button to complete your registration.
How to Reset Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password and need to reset it, here's what you should do:

  1. Click the "Login" button at the top right corner of the page.
  2. On the login screen, scroll to the bottom and click the "Forgot password?" link.
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
  4. The system will send a password reset link to your email inbox.
  5. Wait 1-2 minutes for the email to arrive.
  6. Open the email and click the password reset link.
  7. Enter your new password and click "Submit" to update your password.
How to Contact Support?

If you need help, please use the "Contact Us" section located at the bottom of this page to reach our support team. They are available to assist with any issues or questions you may have regarding your account or our platform.
